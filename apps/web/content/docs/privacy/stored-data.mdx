Phase is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This page explains where your data is stored, how long we keep it, and your rights under GDPR.

## Data Storage Location

All Phase Analytics data is stored in **Nuremberg, Germany** in secure, GDPR-compliant data centers.

<Callout type="info">
We use European data centers to ensure compliance with EU data protection regulations and provide fast access for our global user base.
</Callout>

## Data Retention

**Retention Period:** 1 year for all membership plans

- Analytics data (events, sessions, screen views) is retained for 12 months from the date of collection
- After 1 year, data is automatically and permanently deleted from our systems
- This applies to all plans including free and paid subscriptions

<Callout type="warn">
Once data is deleted after the retention period, it cannot be recovered. Export your data before the retention period expires if you need to keep it longer.
</Callout>

## Data Usage and Protection

**We never sell your data.** Your analytics data is:

- Used solely for providing analytics services to you
- Never shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes
- Never sold to data brokers or other companies
- Protected with industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest)
- Accessible only to you and your authorized team members

## Your GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

<Accordions>
<Accordion title="Right to Access">
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

**How to request:** Email [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh) with "Data Access Request" in the subject line.

**Processing time:** We will provide your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

**Format:** Data will be provided in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)">
You can request deletion of your personal data at any time.

**How to request:** Email [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh) with "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. Include your account email and project ID.

**Processing time:** We will complete the deletion within 30 days of receiving your request.

**What gets deleted:**
- All analytics data associated with your account
- User identification data
- Project settings and configurations
- Team member associations

**Note:** Some data may be retained for legal compliance (billing records, audit logs) as required by law.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Rectification">
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

**How to request:** Email [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh) with details of the data that needs correction.

**Processing time:** Corrections will be made within 30 days.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Data Portability">
You can request your data in a portable, machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

**How to request:** Email [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh) with "Data Portability Request" in the subject line.

**Processing time:** Data will be provided within 30 days.

**Format:** JSON or CSV format, compatible with common analytics platforms.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Restrict Processing">
You can request that we limit how we process your data while a complaint or correction is being resolved.

**How to request:** Email [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh) with details of your restriction request.

**Processing time:** Restrictions will be applied within 7 days.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Right to Object">
You can object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

**How to request:** Email [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh) explaining the basis for your objection.

**Processing time:** We will respond within 30 days and stop processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds.
</Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal data:

- We will notify you within **72 hours** of becoming aware of the breach
- Notification will include details of the breach, potential impact, and measures taken
- We will report the breach to relevant supervisory authorities as required by GDPR

## Contact for Data Requests

For any data-related requests or questions, contact us at:

**Email:** [support@phase.sh](mailto:support@phase.sh)

**Response time:** We aim to respond to all requests within 48 hours and fulfill them within the timeframes specified above.
